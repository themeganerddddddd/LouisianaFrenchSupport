Frequently Asked Questions

  • The app is not loading properly. Try closing and reopening the app, then make sure you are using the latest version.
  • Audio is not playing. Check that your device volume is on and that silent mode is not blocking playback.
  • Progress seems missing. Try logging out and back in, or reinstall the app if the issue continues.
  • I found a mistake in a word or lesson. Please email support with the lesson name and the correction.

About the App

The Louisiana French app is designed to help users learn and engage with Louisiana French through vocabulary, lessons, and interactive study tools.
