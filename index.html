Frequently Asked Questions

  • The app is not loading properly. Close and reopen the app, then make sure you are using the latest version.
  • Audio is not playing. Check your device volume and make sure silent mode is not preventing playback.
  • I found an error in a lesson or word. Please email support with the lesson name and the correction.
  • The app is not responding correctly. Try restarting the app or reinstalling it.
